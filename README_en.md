## 📖 Overview

Anthropic is the world's most cautious AI company — and simultaneously the world's most disruptive.

Claude Code reached $1 billion in annual revenue within six months of launch. Cowork triggered a $285 billion sell-off in enterprise software stocks. MCP was donated to the Linux Foundation and became the industry standard for AI connectivity. In the same month the company refused a Pentagon contract on ethical grounds, it reached #1 on the App Store.

Why don't "caution" and "disruption" contradict each other?

This book **dissects** the company. Origin (PBC / LTBT), Philosophy (Constitutional AI / Mechanistic Interpretability), Technology (Haiku / Sonnet / Opus), Products (Claude Code / Cowork / MCP), Economics (Anthropic Economic Index), Governance (RSP / ethical branding). Six layers, structurally analyzed — revealing that everything is connected by a single, coherent design.

**When we dissected the company, a single blueprint emerged. That is the true identity of Anthropic.**

This book is a sequel to [Silence of Intelligence](https://github.com/Leading-AI-IO/silence-of-intelligence), which analyzed the philosophy of Dario Amodei. It expands the scope from "one person's philosophy" to "the full anatomy of the enterprise."

## 🏗️ Structure

This book consists of six chapters.

| Ch. | Title | What It Dissects |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | The Split — The Structural Inevitability of Anthropic's Birth | Origin. The contradiction of the Scaling Laws discoverer warning about the dangers of scaling. The design of PBC and LTBT |
| 2 | Constitutional AI — The Thought Experiment of Giving AI a Constitution | Philosophy. The limits of RLHF, the principle → self-critique → revision loop, Claude's Character, Mechanistic Interpretability |
| 3 | The Model Architecture — Haiku, Sonnet, Opus | Technology. The design philosophy of the three-tier structure, breaking "bigger = better" twice, Extended Thinking, Computer Use, Agent Team |
| 4 | The Product Trinity — Claude Code, Cowork, MCP | Products. Seizing the terminal, the desktop, and the protocol layer. The unified strategy of "execution, not conversation" |
| 5 | The Economic Index — The Company That Measures Its Own Destructive Power | Economics. Clio, five economic primitives, penetration into 49% of occupations, hiring slowdown for ages 22-25, the gap of "measuring the crisis without prescribing a cure" |
| 6 | The Deliberate Company — Why Anthropic Is Both the Most Cautious and the Most Disruptive | Governance. RSP v3.0, the Pentagon refusal → App Store #1 paradox, the flywheel structure, resolving the paradox of caution and disruption |
